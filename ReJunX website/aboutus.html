About Us

ReJunx is your go-to app for smart and simple e-waste recycling. We connect you with nearby recyclers to help you dispose of your old electronics responsibly. Our app uses advanced technology to assess the value of your e-waste, making sure you get the most out of your recycling efforts. Plus, our friendly chatbot is here to guide you through the process and answer any questions. With ReJunx, recycling is not only easy but also rewarding, as you contribute to a greener planet while earning benefits for your next tech purchase.

E-waste: Reuse, Repair, Recycle - Towards zero waste.

We are committed to reducing E-waste and promote sustainabilty. Join us in our journey to create a better plannet.
